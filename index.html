How To Play

- Press or click anywhere to start. The game will give the first sound and flicker. Repeat the sound by pressing the same color lens.

- The game will play another sound. Repeat these two sounds by pressing the same color lenses, in order.

- Continue repeating the same sequence of sounds correctly.

- If you fail to repeat a sequence exactly, you hear a "RAZZ" sound. This means you've lost. Reload the page to restart.
